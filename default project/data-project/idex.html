Metro fair evasion occurs daily despite new gate placement

By Madeline Seck


Over a hundred fair evasions are documented daily, which make up the highest and most common crime in D.C., Maryland and Virginia’s Metrorail system based on the Metro Transit Police Department (MTPD) crime statistics.

Fair evasion incidents in Prince George’s County's 15 Metrorail stations occur approximately over 300 times a month. Prince George’s County fair evasion incidents make up about 29 percent of all Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) calculated from MTPD’s monthly blotter summaries on documented fare evasions, which include bus, rail and parking lot facilities.
